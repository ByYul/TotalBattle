🍖 Food Resource Management

Having trouble keeping your troops fed? Always in the red with food balance? Here's what you can do:

  • 🗺️ Relocate: Move your city to a land tile with 90% food bonus and Religious Tractate bonus.
  • 📈 Activate Personal Bonus: Turn on the +100% food production personal bonus.
  • 🎯 Hero Talent Points: Reallocate hero points into food production and tile bonus boost.
  • 🏠 Rebuild for efficiency: Destroy some Mansions and build more Food Farms.
  • 👩‍🌾 Use Captain Helen: Level her up and assign her to your city.
  • 🛡️ Equip for Economy: Use your best economy gear (with food boost) on your hero or Helen.
  • 🏹 Before training troops: Make sure Helen or the economic captain is stationed in your city with gear equipped before troop production.
