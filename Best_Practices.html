🎯 Best Practices & Common Sense

  • 🗣️ Communicate: Always inform your clanmates or leaders if you're planning something that might affect them or the clan, like opening a portal or triggering a war.
  • 🏰 Protect assets: Don’t hoard resources or leave troops in city during events — use forts and clan buildings smartly.
  • 💬 No toxic behavior: Trash talk, constant complaints or accusations ruin the fun. Stay civil and support your team.
  • 📚 Learn game mechanics: Read event rules and guides. Ask for help instead of guessing.
  • 🤝 Help clanmates: Donate, assist with building and marches, and support weaker members during KvK or CoT.
  • 🔐 Keep shields up when inactive: Especially during war events. Don't let the enemy farm you for easy points.
  • ⚖️ Play fair: Don’t exploit loopholes or bait players into violating rules, even if technically allowed.
  • 🛡️ Stay protected: Your kingdom is always exposed to external threats. Never leave excess resources unprotected in your city.
  • ⚔️ Act like it's war: If unshielded, treat every moment like it’s a KvK or CoT event — send troops to forts and hide your resources smartly.
  • 📜 Follow ROE: One selfish move can create problems for your clan and friends. Know and follow the Rules of Engagement.
  • 🪞 Golden rule: “Do not do to others what you wouldn’t want done to you.”
  • 🔍 Scout first: If you farm resources from other kingdoms, always scout first. It’s faster, safer, and more respectful.
  • 🚫 No low-might targets: Do not attack cities under 100k Might — not in your kingdom, nor others.
  • 🏯 Respect buildings: Do not attack clan buildings.
  • ⛏️ Respect mines: Do not attack mines outside your clan.
